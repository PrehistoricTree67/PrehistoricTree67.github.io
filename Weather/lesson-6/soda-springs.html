Soda Springs Aquatic Hotel

Come discover the most luxurious hotel in Soda Springs. The 10/10 star-rated underwater hotel has the entrance way where you would see the swans swimming around the geysor fountains along with the dining room and near the cafeteria. The indoor geysor fountain erupts almost every 15 minutes. Each room will be able to see and to discover varieties of whales, dolphins, seals, sea leopards, sea lions, and penguins that will help you to sleep better throughout the night and to have the best dreams you could've ever have or imagined. In the daytime at around 9-11 a.m. customers who stayed in our hotels are able to feed the animals. We will also serve breakfast to customers as well. At 4-6 p.m. we can allow anyone who stays in our hotel to pet the swans. We will even allow to hold the cygnets as well.
