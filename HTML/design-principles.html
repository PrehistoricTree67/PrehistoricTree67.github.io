Design Principles

Jonah Li

White Space

Mcdonald's
Mcdonalds.com

Mcdonald's utilizes white space to make a clear distinction between different products and to promote balance and rhythm for customers to easily navigate through products.


Repetition

Krispy Kreme
Krispykreme.com

The use of color red and green throughout the entire krispykreme design reinforces its brand identity and creates a sense of movement and consistency.

Visual Hierarchy

Popeyes
Popeyes.com

Popeyes applies visual hierarchy principle in this picture. The first thing that draws the attention is the 10 pieces fried chicken, followed by salad and mashed potatoes, and then biscuits. The 'Order Now' button is also a prominant part as it applies solid color to draw attention.
