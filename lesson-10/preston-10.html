Preston, Idaho Lunar New Year

Preston, Idaho will be hosting a Lunar New Year for 15 days starting from January 22nd all the way to February 6th. There will be food, drinks, and some Chinese, Japanese, and Korean snacks and desserts on the table. We will pass out the red envelopes that contains $5 to everyone at the party. We will start the fireworks at 7 p.m. and will keep going for 2 weeks.
