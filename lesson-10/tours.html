Cozumel Aquarium

Our Cozumel aquarium is a place where the world is filled with sea creatures where you can get to discover and to interact with our sea creatures including the seals, sea lions, whales, etc. You will even get to hold starfishes to take it as a souveniour pet.

Exotic Petting Zoo in the City

We want to give our tourist the experience to interact with the exotic animals in the petting zoo. You will even get to choose any of our exotic animals to take for a walk only around and near our local area. Feeding our animals cost $10/person or $100/person when bringing your own food.
